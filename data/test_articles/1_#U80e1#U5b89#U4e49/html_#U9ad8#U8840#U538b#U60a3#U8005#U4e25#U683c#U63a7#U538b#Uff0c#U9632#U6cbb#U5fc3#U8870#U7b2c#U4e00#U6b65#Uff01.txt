                            <p><span style="font-family:宋体"></span></p><p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align:left;margin: 0cm 0cm 0pt; text-indent: 24pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0;"><span style="font-family: " font-size:="" mso-bidi-font-family:="">有研究表明，<!--HAODF:8:gaoxueya--><a href="https://m.haodf.com/citiao/jibing-gaoxueya.html" style="color:#4ba2ed;text-decoration:underline;">高血压</a><!--HAODF:/8:gaoxueya-->患者发生<a href="https://m.haodf.com/citiao/jibing-xinshuai.html" style="color:#4ba2ed;text-decoration:underline;">心衰</a>的风险要比普通人高<span lang="EN-US">6</span>倍！长期的<!--HAODF:8:gaoxueya-->高血压<!--HAODF:/8:gaoxueya-->状态，会增加心脏泵血的负担，进而导致心室肥大，影响心脏舒张功能；再加上<!--HAODF:8:gaoxueya-->高血压<!--HAODF:/8:gaoxueya-->患者往往合并<!--HAODF:8:guanxinbing-->冠心病<!--HAODF:/8:guanxinbing-->，加重心肌缺血缺氧，因此，心衰的发生风险会更高。那么对于<!--HAODF:8:gaoxueya-->高血压<!--HAODF:/8:gaoxueya-->合并心衰的患者，该怎么治疗呢？<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p><span style="font-family:宋体"></span></p><p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align:left;margin: 0cm 0cm 0pt; text-indent: 24pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0;"><strong><span style="color: blue; font-family: " font-size:="" mso-bidi-font-family:="">合理降压，控制血压水平<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></strong></p><p><span style="font-family:宋体"></span></p><p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align:left;margin: 0cm 0cm 0pt; text-indent: 24pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0;"><span style="font-family: " font-size:="" mso-bidi-font-family:="">● <strong>明确降压目标<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></strong></span></p><p><span style="font-family:宋体"></span></p><p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align:left;margin: 0cm 0cm 0pt; text-indent: 24pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0;"><span style="font-family: " font-size:="" mso-bidi-font-family:="">如果患者是单纯的<!--HAODF:8:gaoxueya-->高血压<!--HAODF:/8:gaoxueya-->合并心衰，没有合并其它疾病，目标血压是<span lang="EN-US">140/90
                mmHg</span>；如果患者还合并<!--HAODF:8:tangniaobing-->糖尿病<!--HAODF:/8:tangniaobing-->，目标血压是<span lang="EN-US">130/80 mmHg</span>；如果患者同时合并肾功能衰竭，目标血压则应设为<span lang="EN-US">120/70 mmHg</span>。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p><span style="font-family:宋体"></span></p><p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align:left;margin: 0cm 0cm 0pt; text-indent: 24pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0;"><span style="font-family: " font-size:="" mso-bidi-font-family:="">一般情况下，推荐患者选用普利类（<span lang="EN-US">ACEI</span>类）或沙坦类（<span lang="EN-US">ARB</span>类）降压药，因为这两类药物在降压的同时，还能防止心脏增大，甚至逆转左心室肥厚、缩小心腔、延缓心衰，对心脏有强大的保护作用。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p><span style="font-family:宋体"></span></p><p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align:left;margin: 0cm 0cm 0pt; text-indent: 24pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0;"><span style="font-family: " font-size:="" mso-bidi-font-family:="">● <strong>监测降压效果<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></strong></span></p><p><span style="font-family:宋体"></span></p><p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align:left;margin: 0cm 0cm 0pt;
